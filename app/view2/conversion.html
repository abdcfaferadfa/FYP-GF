From ` Number ` To ` Polynomial `

A number can be convert to a Polynomial in a similar way as a radix conversion of a number.

Take ` GF(2) ` as example, 13 is ` 1101_2` in base 2, and can be represented as `x^3+x^2+1` in `GF(2)`

Below is a live demo of how a number could be convert into a polynomial representation under Galois Field. Change the number below to see how other numbers are converted. In addition, you can open up the

upper right corner

to change the

base

radix

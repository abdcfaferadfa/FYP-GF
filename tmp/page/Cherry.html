Cherry Ripe slice:

This classic Cherry Ripe Slice Recipe is from myself and it was one of my favourite slices when I was growing up! Although I’m also a big fan of my No Bake Cherry Ripe Slice recipe, this classic Cherry Ripe Slice is just that little bit special thanks to being loaded with glace cherries, biscuit pieces, coconut and of course, a thick layer of dark chocolate. I made this Cherry Ripe Slice in a 20cm square cake tin to help make it a little bit higher (and give you more slice in each piece!), however you can also make it in a conventional 28x18cm slice tin too.
